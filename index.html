The potential risks of hiring services to write your assignments

    You could get a poor-quality paper:

    Most writers on essay writing services are not native English speakers. You could get a paper full of plagiarism, poor grammar, doesn’t follow instructions, poor formatting, poor punctuation. They could even sell a paper that was previously written for someone else, only to find out much later, after the professor calls you in, and you have no explanation

    Your private information could be released

    This is the worst absolute nightmare. What if some of your schoolmates see your details on such a site? What if it ends up on the professor’s desk, and then you are caught for academic plagiarism? Your program is terminated there and then

    No options to customize your paper:

    Most services are doing large-scale ‘production’ of assignments, and, they have no time to customize your assignment to look like your own paper. What if they give you a paper that is way above your writing standard, for example, if English is your second language, and then the professor detects something wrong? And then you get caught for handing in work that is not your own?

    How expensive will it be, OVER TIME?

    They key is the word, “over time”. You could pay for one paper, and get it done. How about through the semester, for all the work? How much will it come to? Will you be able to afford?

    Guarantee:

    You could pay for a paper and get one, then hand it in, only for the grades to come back a week or 2 weeks later. You lose all the money you paid. How do you get compensated for that? Because, you have just lost your money, time and grades. How sad!
